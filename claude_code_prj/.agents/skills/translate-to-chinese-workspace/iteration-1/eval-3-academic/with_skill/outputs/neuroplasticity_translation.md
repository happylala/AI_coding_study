# 神经可塑性学术文本翻译

**原文：**
Neuroplasticity refers to the brain's remarkable ability to reorganize itself by forming new neural connections throughout life.

**译文：**
神经可塑性/neuroplasticity 是指大脑在整个生命周期中通过形成新的神经连接/neural connections 来重组/重新组织 (reorganize) 自身的非凡能力。

---

**原文：**
This adaptive mechanism enables the nervous system to compensate for injury and disease, and to adjust activities in response to new situations or changes in the environment.

**译文：**
这种适应机制/适应性机制 (adaptive mechanism) 使神经系统/nervous system 能够补偿/代偿 (compensate) 损伤和疾病带来的影响。它还能根据新情况或环境变化来调整活动。

---

**原文：**
Recent studies have demonstrated that neuroplasticity is not limited to childhood development but continues well into adulthood, challenging long-held assumptions about the fixed nature of the adult brain.

**译文：**
近期研究表明，神经可塑性并不局限于儿童发育阶段。它在成年期仍然持续存在。这一发现挑战了长期以来关于成年大脑固定性/不变性 (fixed nature) 的假设。
